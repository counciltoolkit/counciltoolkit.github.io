Why use it?

  • It's responsive - content, context and actions for all device types have been considered from the outset.
  • I's fast - move from prototype to final release with ease.
  • It's flexible - it will provide for a degree of customisation to suit your Council's brand and CMS, although a pre-rolled Umbraco instance is provided for those who may need it.

Case study

The Challenge

Storm ID has been working with City of Edinburgh Council to support them in the development of the ICT & Digital transformation programme. The focus of the work has been working in partnership to create a fit for purpose technical architecture to deliver information and transactional digital services across mobile, tablet and desktop.

The Solution

Storm ID has developed a closed alpha version of the City of Edinburgh Council web site using analytics data to focus on meeting the needs of different users and stakeholders of CEC. The Alpha site, cover in The Scotsman, was developed using an agile methodology.

The Results

The new CEC website will be launched in Spring 2014.
