Why use Council Toolkit?

It's highly responsive - Content, context and actions have been considered from the outset. Templates for typical content types have been developed for all manner of devices from small screens on smartphones and tablets through to Internet TV.

It's fast - Rapid prototyping is easy, with many user interface elements, styles and tried and tested patterns baked in for you to create with. Move from prototype to release candidate with ease.

It's flexible - It will provide for a degree of customisation to suit your Council's brand and CMS, although a pre-rolled Umbraco instance is provided for those who may need it.
